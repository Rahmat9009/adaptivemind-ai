# AdaptiveMind AI

**Tagline:**
> The AI tutor that learns how you learn.

---

# Vision

AdaptiveMind AI is an intelligent educational platform that personalizes learning for every student.

Instead of giving every learner the same explanation, the platform continuously adapts its teaching style, quizzes, and study plans based on each student's learning preferences and performance.

Our mission is to make learning feel like having a personal tutor who understands how you think.

---

# Problem

Current AI tutors are excellent at answering questions but poor at adapting to individual learning styles.

Students often receive explanations that don't match how they understand information.

This leads to frustration, slower learning, and lower engagement.

---

# Solution

AdaptiveMind AI builds a learning profile for every student.

The AI observes:

- preferred explanation style
- quiz performance
- repeated mistakes
- mastered concepts
- learning pace

It continuously updates this profile to personalize future lessons.
